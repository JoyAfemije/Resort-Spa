Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 7 days or more before check-in receive a full refund. Cancellations within 7 days are subject to a one-night charge.

Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes, we provide complimentary airport transfers for all guests. Please provide your flight details at least 48 hours before arrival.

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in is at 3:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request.

Are pets allowed?

We welcome small pets (under 25 lbs) with advance notice. A pet fee of $50 per night applies. Service animals are always welcome.

Is WiFi available?

Complimentary high-speed WiFi is available throughout the resort, including all guest rooms and public areas.

Do you have facilities for events and weddings?

Yes! We have beautiful venues for weddings, conferences, and special events. Contact our events team for customized packages.
